An Earth-Based Game Company

The time is August of 1984 and the place is Raider Bay, a fictional fishing town where times have gotten rough. But there is hope in the bay at last. Gerald F. Beardsman, the greatest angler to have ever fished these waters has died, and he is leaving $452.6 Million to the first crew to win his contest.

Grab a crew and sail your ship into the bay in search of fish and treasure. Upgrade your boat, defend against pirates, and obtain all 5 trophies to win!
